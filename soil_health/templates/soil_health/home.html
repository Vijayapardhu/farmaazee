Soil Testing Guide
Testing Steps
  1. Collect soil samples from different areas of your field
  2. Mix the samples thoroughly in a clean container
  3. Air dry the soil for 24-48 hours
  4. Send to a certified soil testing laboratory
  5. Follow the recommendations based on test results
Key Parameters
  • pH Level: Determines nutrient availability
  • Organic Matter: Improves soil structure
  • Nitrogen (N): Essential for plant growth
  • Phosphorus (P): Important for root development
  • Potassium (K): Helps with disease resistance
